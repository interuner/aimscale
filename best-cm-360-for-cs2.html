CS2 guide

Best cm/360 for CS2.

CS2 rewards crosshair placement, small corrections, and predictable movement. Many players are comfortable in a medium to slow cm/360 range, but the best setting is the one you can control under pressure.

Practical Starting Ranges

RangeFeelCommon tradeoff
25 to 40 cmFastEasy clearing and turning, but can make pixel corrections harder.
40 to 65 cmBalancedOften comfortable for rifling, angle holding, and controlled sprays.
65 cm and higherSlowVery stable aim, but requires more arm movement and desk space.

How To Test A CS2 Sensitivity

  • Check whether you can clear common angles without lifting too often.
  • Spray, counter-strafe, and adjust onto small targets before deciding.
  • Keep the same setting for several sessions before judging it too harshly.

Convert From Valorant

If you are moving from Valorant, start with the Valorant to CS2 converter. If the result feels too heavy for fast peeks, lower your cm/360 slightly by raising sensitivity in small steps.
