Privacy Policy

How AimScale handles data.

Last updated: May 8, 2026. This policy describes how AimScale handles information when you visit the site, use the calculator, or contact us.

Information We Process

AimScale does not require accounts. The calculator runs in your browser. Values such as sensitivity, DPI, selected games, and precision are processed locally by the page script and are not intentionally sent to AimScale by the calculator.

Like most websites, our hosting and security providers may process technical request data, such as IP address, user agent, requested URL, referring page, timestamps, and similar logs needed to deliver, secure, and troubleshoot the site.

Contact Messages

If you contact us by email or GitHub issue, we receive the information you choose to provide, such as your message, account name, email address, and technical details related to your request.

Cookies, Analytics, And Advertising

The current calculator does not need cookies to work. We may use privacy, security, analytics, or advertising services as the site grows.

  • Cloudflare may use cookies or similar technologies for security, traffic management, and abuse prevention.
  • If Google Analytics is added, it may collect usage information to help us understand pages, search traffic, and site performance.
  • If Google AdSense or other advertising is added, ad partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve, measure, and limit ads.

For users in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, Switzerland, or other regions where consent is required, we intend to request consent before using non-essential cookies or personalized advertising.

Service Providers

We may use third-party providers to host, secure, analyze, index, and monetize the site. These may include Cloudflare, GitHub, Google Search Console, Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and similar services. Their processing is governed by their own policies and agreements.

Retention

Technical logs and analytics data are retained according to the settings and retention periods of the service providers we use. Contact messages may be retained as long as needed to respond, maintain records, and improve the site.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies in your browser, use privacy controls provided by advertising and analytics partners, or contact us about privacy requests. Some browser or regional controls may affect how ads, analytics, and security services work.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the contact options on our contact page.
