Valorant guide

Best cm/360 for Valorant.

There is no single best Valorant sensitivity. A practical starting window for many tactical FPS players is often somewhere around medium to slow cm/360, then tuned for comfort and desk space.

Practical Starting Ranges

RangeFeelCommon tradeoff
25 to 35 cmFastQuick turns and flicks, but small corrections can feel sensitive.
35 to 55 cmBalancedA useful middle ground for angle clearing, tracking, and micro-adjustments.
55 to 75 cmSlowStable crosshair placement, but wide turns need more arm movement.

How To Choose

  • If you over-flick and miss small corrections, try a higher cm/360.
  • If clearing angles feels heavy or you run out of mousepad, try a lower cm/360.
  • Change one thing at a time: either DPI or in-game sensitivity, not both.
